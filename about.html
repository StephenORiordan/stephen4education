Who Am I?

I'm a final year Physics and Applied Maths student. I've been engaged with student representation since I started University. I was a Class Rep in 1st year and have since served 3 terms on the SU Executive. I was first elected as the SEFS College Rep and then have been the Student Council Chairperson for the last 2 years. These positions have given me a wealth of experience in advocating for students and have afforded me the opportunity to learn how the SU operates in detail.

I've also been extensively involved with societies. This last year I sat on the Societies Executive as the Trainings & Accessibility Officer and I've held over a dozen positions within societies. This has given me experience running events and building a team.
