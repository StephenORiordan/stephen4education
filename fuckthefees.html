Manifesto Pledges

Fuck the Fees

Hidden Costs/Repeat Fees: As if college wasn’t expensive enough already, it is incredibly stressful to be hit with additional charges durings the course of your studies such as late book fees, repeat exam fees etc. It’s important that both students are aware of these charges and that the Union works towards reducing these fees. That’s what I plan on doing if elected.

Capped Results: The principle of capping repeat exams is a relatively new one in Ireland and something that isn’t broadly done across European universities. It’s an unfair limitation on people who simply performed worse in the Summer/Winter assessments. The mitigation process helps to alleviate some of this limitation but I plan on pushing for a complete removal of capped results in line with the European standard.

Education For All: There’s little point talking about education within University without talking about the barriers to access 3rd level in the first place. University shouldn’t be a place for only those that can afford it or don’t have additional caregiving requirements. It’s important that we make education as accessible as possible. We need to fight for an increase in the eligibility criteria for SUSI and expand access routes such as HEAR and DARE to afford everyone the opportunity to attend college.
